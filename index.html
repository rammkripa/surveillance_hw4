Airplane Mode Artwork

As we embark on our journeys through the skies, we often believe that activating "airplane mode" disconnects us from the digital world and shields our privacy. However, this artwork, titled "Airplane Mode," invites you to explore the illusion of disconnection. In this piece, you will hear a familiar flight safety announcement while witnessing the experience of logging onto airplane WiFi, only to confront a cookie consent banner that limits your options and subtly tracks your online presence.
